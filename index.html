About Us

ProGuide Research Hub Zambia provides structured, professional academic writing and research support to college and university students. We specialize in guiding students through the research process with clarity, academic rigor, and ethical standards.

All services are managed by a qualified Research Consultant and are designed to support learning, understanding, and proper academic presentation.

Our Services

  • Full proposal development (Chapter 1–3)
  • Proposal editing and restructuring
  • Topic refinement and feasibility assessment
  • Problem statement, objectives, and research questions

2. Guided Proposal & Thesis Writing

  • Step-by-step academic guidance
  • Research templates and examples
  • Methodology explanation and justification
  • Supervisor comment correction support

3. Assignments & Coursework Support

  • Essays, reports, and case studies

4. Referencing & Academic Editing

5. Research Instruments & Data Support

6. Proposal Defense & Presentation Support

  • PowerPoint proposal defense preparation

  • Anticipated questions and guidance

7. Academic Consultations

  • One-on-one research consultations
  • Topic selection and refinement
  • Supervisor feedback interpretation
